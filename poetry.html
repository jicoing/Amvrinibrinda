Monsoon Memories

By Anika Chatterjee, Kolkata

Amber pools in concrete oceans
Guarding dreams of pavement sleepers
While hungry meters count the hours
Until dawn's charitable light

Streetlight Sonnet

By Rajiv Das, Student Contributor

Twenty-six letters swim
In broth of shared humanity
Vowels nourish, consonants sustain
Literacy's recipe revealed
